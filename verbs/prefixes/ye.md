# ye·

`ye·` is a [verb](../index.md) prefix, marking a feminine singular subject (optionally accompanied by a neuter singular object).

Examples:

> <mark>Ye</mark>·[nuhweʔ](../n/nuhwe_.md)·s. *‘She likes it.’ ([Ormston 1993](../../texts/Ormston1993.md#ex-1-4))*
>
> <mark>Ye</mark>·nuhweʔ·s ne athereʔ. *‘She likes the basket.’ ([Ormston 1993](../../texts/Ormston1993.md#ex-1-4))*
> 
> <mark>Ye</mark>·[rok](../r/rok.md)·s. *‘She chops.’ ([Ormston 1993](../../texts/Ormston1993.md#ex-1-3))*
