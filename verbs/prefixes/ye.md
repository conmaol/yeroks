# ye·

`ye·` is a [verb](../index.md) prefix, marking a feminine singular subject (optionally accompanied by a neuter singular object).

Examples:

> <mark>Ye</mark>·[nuhweʔ](../n/nuhwe_.md)·s. *([Ormston 1993](../../texts/Ormston1993.md#ex-1-4) – ‘She likes it.’)*
>
> <mark>Ye</mark>·nuhweʔ·s ne athereʔ. *([Ormston 1993](../../texts/Ormston1993.md#ex-1-4) – ‘She likes the basket.’)*
> 
> <mark>Ye</mark>·[rok](../r/rok.md)·s. *([Ormston 1993](../../texts/Ormston1993.md#ex-1-3) — ‘She chops.’)*
