# ye·

`ye·` is a [verb](../index.md) prefix marking a feminine singular subject.

Examples:

> <mark>Ye</mark>[nuhweʔs](../n/nuhwe_.md) ne athereʔ. *([Ormston 1993](../../texts/Ormston1993.md#fn-2-5-i) – ‘She likes the basket.’)*
> 
> <mark>Ye</mark>nuhweʔs. *([Ormston 1993](../../texts/Ormston1993.md#fn-2-5-i) – ‘She likes it.’)*
>
> <mark>Ye</mark>[roks](../r/rok.md). *([Ormston 1993](../../texts/Ormston1993.md#ex-1-3) — ‘She chops.’)*
