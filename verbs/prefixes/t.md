# ·t·

`·t·` is a [verb](../index.md) prefix marking either:
- a first person singular subject
- duplicative verbs
- cislocative verbs

## Agreement

See also: **[·k·](k.md)**

Examples:

> <mark>T</mark>[yvʔthos](../y/yv_tho.md). *([Ormston 1993](../../texts/Ormston1993.md#ex-1-3) — ‘I plant’.)*

## Duplicative

Examples:

> Waʔ<mark>t</mark>[kahriʔneʔ](../h/hri_.md). *([Ormston 1993](../../texts/Ormston1993.md#ex-1-6) – ‘It broke.’)*
>
> v<mark>te</mark>[wasvʔneʔ](../a/asv_.md) *([Ormston 1993](../../texts/Ormston1993.md#ex-1-6) – ‘It will fall.’)*

## Cislocative

Examples:

> Sak <mark>t</mark>[ahwkwatatweyaʔtvʔ](../a/ataweya_t.md). *([Ormston 1993](../../texts/Ormston1993.md#ex-1-7) – ‘Sak broke in on me.’)*
