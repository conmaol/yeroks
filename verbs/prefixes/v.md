# v·

`v·` is a [verb](../index.md) prefix, marking future mood.

Examples:

> Thikv kaʔsere <mark>v</mark>[horyohteʔ](../r/ryo.md) ne yaʔniha. *([Ormston 1993](../../texts/Ormston1993.md#ex-1-4) – ‘That car will be the death of your father.’)*
>
> <mark>v</mark>[tewasvʔneʔ](../a/asv_.md) *([Ormston 1993](../../texts/Ormston1993.md#ex-1-6) – ‘It will fall.’)*
