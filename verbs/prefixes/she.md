# she·

`she·` is a verb prefix, marking the combination of a second person singular subject and a feminine singular object.

Examples:

> <mark>She</mark>[kvs](../k/kv.md). *([Ormston 1993](../../texts/Ormston1993.md#ex-1-4) – ‘You see her.’)*
