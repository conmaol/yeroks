# ho·

`ho·` is a [verb](../index.md) prefix, marking a masculine singular object, optionally accompanying a neuter singular subject.

See also: **[ro·](ro.md)**

Intransitive examples:

> Wa<mark>ho</mark>[yoʔtvʔ](../y/yo_tv.md). *([Ormston 1993](../../texts/Ormston1993.md#ex-1-3) – ‘He worked.’)*

Transitive examples:

> Thikv kaʔsere v<mark>ho</mark>[ryohteʔ](../r/ryo.md) ne yaʔniha. *([Ormston 1993](../../texts/Ormston1993.md#ex-1-4) – ‘That car will be the death of your father.’)*
