# hakw·

`hakw·` is a [verb](../index.md) prefix, marking a masculine singular subject accompanies by a first-person singular object.

Examples:

> Sak ta<mark>hwkw</mark>[atatweyaʔt](../a/ataweya_t.md)vʔ. *([Ormston 1993](../../texts/Ormston1993.md#ex-1-7) – ‘Sak broke in on me.’)*
