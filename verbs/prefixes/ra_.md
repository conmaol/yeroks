# ra·

`ra·` is a [verb](../index.md) prefix marking a masculine singular subject.

Examples:

> Sak <mark>ra</mark>[nuhweʔ]()s akotyaʔtawi. *([Ormston 1993](../../texts/Ormston1993.md#ex-1-1) – ‘Sak likes her dress’)*
> 
> <mark>Ra</mark>nuhweʔs. *([Ormston 1993](../../texts/Ormston1993.md#ex-1-1) – ‘He likes it’)*
