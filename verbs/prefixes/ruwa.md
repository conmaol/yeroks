# ruwa·

`ruwa·` is a [verb](../index.md) prefix, marking the combination of a feminine singular subject and a masculine singular object.

Other spellings: maybe `ronwa·`

Examples:

> <mark>Ruwa</mark>·[nuhweʔ](../n/nuhwe_.md)·s. *‘She likes him.’ ([Ormston 1993](../../texts/Ormston1993.md#ex-1-4))*
