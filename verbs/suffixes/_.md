# ·ʔ

`·ʔ` is a [verb](../index.md) suffix marking punctual aspect.

Examples:

> [Waʔkhninu](../h/hninu.md)<mark>ʔ</mark> ne kanaktaʔ. *([Ormston 1993](../../texts/Ormston1993.md#ex-1-2) – ‘I bought the/a bed.’)*
> 
> [Waʔkenaktahninu]<mark>ʔ</mark>(../h/hninu.md). *([Ormston 1993](../../texts/Ormston1993.md#ex-1-2) – ‘I bed-bought.’)*
>
> [Wahoyoʔtv](../y/yo_tv.md)<mark>ʔ</mark>. *([Ormston 1993](../../texts/Ormston1993.md#ex-1-3) – ‘He worked.’)*
