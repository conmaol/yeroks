# ·u

`·u` is a [verb](../index.md) suffix, marking stative aspect.

Note that the underlying subject of the verb is demoted to surface object.

Examples:

> [Ro](../prefixes/ro.md)·[torat](../a/atorat.md)·<mark>u</mark>. *‘He has hunted.’ ([Ormston 1993](../../texts/Ormston1993.md#ex-1-14))*
>
> Ro·[rast](../r/rast.md)·<mark>u</mark>. *‘He has drawn.’ ([Ormston 1993](../../texts/Ormston1993.md#ex-1-14))*
