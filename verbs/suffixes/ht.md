# ·ht·

`·ht·` is a [verb](../index.md) suffix, marking causativity.

Examples:

> Thikv kaʔsere [vhoryo](../r/ryo.md)<mark>ht</mark>eʔ ne yaʔniha. *([Ormston 1993](../../texts/Ormston1993.md#ex-1-4) – ‘That car will be the death of your father.’)*
